Soil Preparation For Sod

There is no better time to enhance your lawn's ultimate beauty and success than before you sod.

Benefits of Proper Soil Preparation
  • Improved Uniformity
  • Increased Density
  • Faster Recovery From Wear
  • Reduced Use of Water, Fertilizer, Chemicals
  • Reduced Maintenance

Tilling allows for optimum root growth. Sod needs sunlight, air, water and nutrients. Simply put, if it's lacking any one of these the sod will suffer. In the right proportions, the grass will flourish, but many soils are compact. By tilling the soil, air and nutrients are stirred up and readily available to the sod. This will allow for rapid new root growth. As long as you can provide the water and sunlight!

The availability and cost of different types of sod grasses is generally found in this order starting with the highest to lowest cost of sod:

  • Zoysia
  • Bermuda - 419 Tifton
  • Shade Tolerant St. Augustine Grass (Bitter Blue)
  • St. Augustine - Floratam
