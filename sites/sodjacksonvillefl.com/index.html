Grass Types We Install

St. Augustine Sod - Florida's most popular grass. Floratam for full sun, Palmetto for shade tolerant areas.

Empire Zoysia - Low maintenance, drought tolerant. Great for front yards that need curb appeal.

Bermuda Sod - Full sun grass with excellent durability. Ideal for high-traffic back yards.

Not sure which grass? We'll recommend the best sod for your property's sun exposure and soil conditions.

Yard Drainage Solutions

Yard holds tons of water? We fix drainage problems before installing new sod so your lawn stays healthy.

French Drain Installation - Channel water away from problem areas in your yard.

Yard Leveling & Grading - Contour your yard for proper drainage control. No more standing water.

Many yards need drainage work before new sod will thrive. We evaluate and fix the problem, then install fresh sod.
