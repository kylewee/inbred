There are many types of sod for lawns. Choosing the right sod is easy because there are many types of sod in Florida that can be grown in shade or full sun.

Shade vs Full Sun Sod

Inadequate consideration is often given to the sunlight requirements of lawn grass sod. Before installing sod, watch your lawn throughout the day and note how many hours of direct sunlight it receives.

Shade Sod: If your lawn gets four hours of sunlight or less, choose shade-tolerant sod like Palmetto St. Augustine.

Full Sun Sod: Requires six or more hours of direct sunlight. Floratam St. Augustine is ideal for full sun.
